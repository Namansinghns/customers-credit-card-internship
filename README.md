1. Project Objective

The objective of this project was to Analyze customer and credit card transaction data to understand customer behaviour, spending patterns, credit utilization, and revenue generation. and provide interactive  insights for better business decision-making.

2. Data Sources
 
 Customer dataset containing demographic details such as age, gender, income, education, and customer satisfaction.
 Credit card dataset containing financial information such as credit limit, transaction amount, utilization ratio, and card category.

3. Dashboard Development Approach

Importing and cleaning data in Power BI.
Creating relationships between customer and credit card tables.
Creating calculated measures using DAX for metrics such as Total Revenue, Transaction Amount, Credit Utilization, and Weekly Revenue.
Designing interactive visualizations including bar charts, line charts, KPI cards, and slicers.
Building separate dashboards for customer insights and credit card performance.

4. Key Insights

Certain credit card categories generated higher revenue compared to others.
Customers with higher income generally had higher credit limits and transaction amounts.
Spending patterns varied across expense types such as bills, entertainment, and travel.
Weekly revenue trends helped identify changes in transaction behaviour.
Customer satisfaction scores showed relationships with spending and card usage.

5.Conclusion

This project demonstrates how credit card data can be leveraged 
to generate actionable insights for financial decision-making and risk management.
